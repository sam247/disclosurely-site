# Analytics Overview

Transform your whistleblowing data into actionable insights with Disclosurely's comprehensive analytics and reporting platform.

## What is Analytics?

Disclosurely Analytics provides a complete suite of tools to monitor, analyze, and report on your organization's whistleblowing program. From real-time dashboards to custom compliance reports, our analytics platform helps you understand trends, identify risks, and demonstrate program effectiveness to stakeholders.

**Key Analytics Capabilities:**
- **Real-Time Dashboards** - Live metrics and KPIs updated as cases progress
- **Trend Analysis** - Identify patterns across time periods, departments, and categories
- **Compliance Reporting** - Pre-built templates for regulatory requirements
- **Custom Report Builder** - Create tailored reports for specific needs
- **Visual Charts** - Interactive charts and graphs for data visualization
- **Export Capabilities** - Download reports in CSV, PDF, and Excel formats
- **AI-Powered Insights** - Pattern detection and risk assessment analytics

## Analytics Components

### 1. Main Analytics Dashboard

The central hub for all your whistleblowing program metrics.

**Access:** Dashboard > Analytics

**Key Features:**
- High-level overview of program health
- Case volume trends (daily, weekly, monthly, yearly)
- Category distribution charts
- Priority and urgency breakdowns
- Response time metrics
- Resolution rate tracking
- Active cases monitoring
- Escalation rate analysis

**Learn More:** [Analytics Dashboard Guide](/analytics/dashboard)

### 2. Case Statistics

Detailed statistics and metrics for case management.

**Metrics Tracked:**
- Total reports received (all-time and filtered periods)
- Average response time to initial acknowledgment
- Average time to resolution
- Case closure rate
- Re-opened cases tracking
- Assignment distribution
- Status distribution
- Department/location analysis

**Learn More:** [Statistics & Metrics](/analytics/statistics)

### 3. Compliance Analytics

Specialized analytics for regulatory compliance and audit reporting.

**Compliance Features:**
- EU Whistleblowing Directive reporting
- SOX compliance metrics
- GDPR data subject request tracking
- Audit trail reporting
- Policy compliance monitoring
- Deadline tracking and alerts
- Regulatory submission templates

**Learn More:** [Compliance Analytics](/analytics/compliance-analytics)

## Key Metrics Explained

### Case Volume Metrics

**Total Reports**
- All reports received across all time periods
- Filterable by date range, status, category
- Includes both anonymous and confidential reports

**Active Cases**
- Reports currently under investigation
- Excludes closed, resolved, and archived cases
- Real-time updates as statuses change

**New Reports (Period)**
- Reports submitted within selected time period
- Compare across different time ranges
- Identify reporting trends and spikes
