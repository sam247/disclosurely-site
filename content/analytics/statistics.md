# Report Statistics

Deep dive into your whistleblowing program's metrics, trends, and performance indicators with comprehensive statistical analysis.

## Overview

Report Statistics provides detailed metrics and analytics beyond the dashboard's high-level overview. Analyze performance trends, compare time periods, track investigator productivity, and identify patterns that inform program improvements.

**Access:** Dashboard > Analytics > Statistics
