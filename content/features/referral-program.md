# Referral Program

Earn valuable rewards by referring other organizations to Disclosurely. Share your unique referral link and unlock account credits, free subscription months, extended storage, and premium features.

## Overview

The Disclosurely Referral Program rewards you for helping other organizations implement effective whistleblowing solutions. When you refer a colleague, partner, or another organization that subscribes to Disclosurely, both you and the referred organization benefit.

**How It Works:**
1. Generate your unique referral link from your dashboard
2. Share with colleagues, partners, and professional network
3. Earn rewards when they subscribe
4. Track referrals and reward status in real-time
5. Redeem rewards for account credits, features, and more

**Access:** Dashboard > Account > Referral Program

**Availability:** All plan levels (Free, Starter, Pro, Enterprise)

**Learn More:** [Subscription Tiers](/features/subscription-tiers)

## How the Referral Program Works

### Getting Your Referral Link

**Generate Unique Link:**

Every Disclosurely account receives a unique referral link:

**How to Access:**
1. Log in to your dashboard
2. Navigate to **Account > Referral Program**
3. Find your unique referral link
4. Copy link or share directly

**Your Referral Link Format:**
```
https://disclosurely.com/signup?ref=YOUR_UNIQUE_CODE
```

**Unique Referral Code:**
- Automatically generated for your account
- Permanent (doesn't change)
- Tracks all referred signups
- Cannot be shared or transferred

**Multiple Links:**
You can create multiple tracking links for different campaigns:
- Link for LinkedIn posts
- Link for email campaigns
- Link for webinars
- Link for partner websites

**Access:** Dashboard > Referral Program > Create Campaign Link

**1. Direct Link Sharing:**

### Who Can You Refer?

**Eligible Referrals:**

✅ **Organizations that can be referred:**
- Companies implementing compliance programs
- Non-profits establishing whistleblowing channels
- Educational institutions
- Government agencies
- Professional service firms
- Industry associations
- Any organization not currently using Disclosurely

✅ **Referral relationships:**
- Professional colleagues and contacts
- Business partners and vendors
- Industry peers and competitors
- Conference connections
- Online community members
- Former colleagues

❌ **Ineligible referrals:**
- Existing Disclosurely customers
- Organizations you're affiliated with as employee/owner

- Self-referrals or fake organizations

**Verification:**
All referrals are verified before rewards are issued to prevent fraud.

### Referral Tracking

**How Referrals Are Tracked:**

**Attribution Methods:**
1. **Unique Link Click** - Cookie stored in browser (30-day window)
2. **Referral Code Entry** - Manual code entry during signup
3. **Email Domain Matching** - Organization domain from invitation email
4. **Admin Verification** - Manual attribution if technical issues

**Tracking Dashboard:**

Monitor your referral activity in real-time:

**Access:** Dashboard > Referral Program > My Referrals

**Metrics Displayed:**
- **Total Clicks** - How many people clicked your link
- **Signups** - How many created free accounts
- **Conversions** - How many subscribed to paid plans
- **Active Referrals** - Currently subscribed organizations
- **Total Rewards Earned** - Lifetime value of rewards
- **Pending Rewards** - Awaiting redemption

**Referral Status:**
- **Clicked** - Someone clicked your link
- **Signed Up** - Created free account
- **Trial Started** - Started paid plan trial
- **Converted** - Subscribed to paid plan
- **Active** - Currently paying customer
- **Churned** - Canceled subscription

**Detailed View:**

Click on any referral to see:
- Organization name (if public)
- Signup date
- Plan type (Starter, Pro, Enterprise)
- Reward earned
- Reward redemption status
- Days active

## Rewards & Benefits

### Reward Structure

**What You Earn:**

Rewards vary based on the plan your referral subscribes to:

**Starter Plan Referrals:**
- **$50 account credit** (one-time)
- **+5 GB storage** added to your account
- **1 month free** on your current plan

**Pro Plan Referrals:**
- **$150 account credit** (one-time)
- **+20 GB storage** added to your account
- **3 months free** on your current plan
- **Early access** to new features (1 quarter)

**Enterprise Plan Referrals:**
- **$500 account credit** (one-time)
- **Unlimited storage** upgrade for 6 months
- **6 months free** on your current plan
- **Early access** to new features (1 year)
- **Dedicated success manager** consultation
- **Conference ticket** (select events)

**Annual Subscription Bonus:**
If your referral chooses annual billing (vs. monthly):
- **+50% bonus** on all rewards
- Example: Pro referral = $225 credit instead of $150

### Recurring Rewards

**Lifetime Value:**

Earn ongoing benefits while your referrals remain active customers:

**Monthly Recurring Rewards:**
- **5% of referral's monthly subscription** as account credit
- Applied automatically each month
- Continues as long as they remain subscribed
- No limit to total lifetime earnings

**Example:**
- Refer 3 organizations on Pro plan ($99/month each)
- Earn: 3 × $99 × 5% = **$14.85/month** recurring credit
- Over 2 years: $14.85 × 24 = **$356.40 total**

**Reward Tiers:**

Unlock increasing benefits as you refer more organizations:

**Bronze (1-2 referrals):**
- Standard rewards listed above
- Referral dashboard access

**Silver (3-5 referrals):**
- +10% bonus on all rewards
- Priority support for your account
- Quarterly product roadmap updates

**Gold (6-10 referrals):**
- +25% bonus on all rewards
- Free upgrade to next plan tier (for duration of referrals)
- Monthly product team office hours access
- Named in referral leaderboard

**Platinum (11+ referrals):**
- +50% bonus on all rewards
- Free Enterprise features regardless of your plan
- Direct product team contact
- Invitation to exclusive events
- Partnership opportunities

### Redemption Options

**How to Use Rewards:**

**1. Account Credits:**
- Applied automatically to your subscription
- Reduces or eliminates monthly bill
- Credits never expire
- Can accumulate unlimited balance

**2. Plan Upgrades:**
- Use credits to upgrade to higher plan
- Example: Use $150 credit to upgrade from Starter ($49) to Pro ($99) for 3 months
- Upgrade remains active while credits available

**3. Storage Expansion:**
- Redeem credits for additional storage
- $25 credit = 50 GB additional storage
- Storage persists for 1 year from redemption

**4. Feature Unlocks:**
- Unlock Enterprise features temporarily
- Example: $100 credit = 1 month Enterprise features
- Access advanced analytics, API, custom branding

**5. Cash Payout (Platinum Tier Only):**
- Minimum $500 balance required
- PayPal or bank transfer
- Paid quarterly
- Tax documentation required (IRS Form W-9 for US)

**6. Charity Donation:**
- Donate credits to supported charities
- 100% of credits donated (Disclosurely matches 10%)
- Tax receipt provided
- Supported causes: transparency, ethics, whistleblower protection

**Redemption Process:**
1. Navigate to **Dashboard > Referral Program > Rewards**
2. View available credit balance
3. Select redemption option
4. Confirm redemption
5. Rewards applied immediately or within 24 hours

### What NOT to Do

❌ **Don't spam:**
- Mass unsolicited emails
- Posting in irrelevant groups
- Aggressive sales tactics
- Repeated messages to same person

❌ **Don't misrepresent:**
- Exaggerate features or capabilities
- Promise specific results
- Claim affiliation you don't have
- Hide that it's a referral link

❌ **Don't violate policies:**
- Share in prohibited channels (competitor forums)
- Violate employer social media policies
- Breach confidentiality or NDAs
- Use misleading advertising

**Eligibility:**
- Must have active Disclosurely account (any plan)

**Prohibited Activities:**

### Tax Implications

**Tax Reporting:**

**United States:**
- Rewards over $600/year may require IRS Form 1099
- You're responsible for reporting as income
- Consult tax professional for guidance

**International:**
- Tax treatment varies by country

- Disclosurely does not provide tax advice

**Business vs. Personal:**
If referring as business entity:
- Different tax treatment may apply
- May need business tax documentation

## Referral Support

### Getting Help

**Referral Program Questions:**

**Contact:** referrals@disclosurely.com

**Common Questions:**

**Q: When do I receive rewards?**
A: Credits applied within 48 hours of referred organization's first payment. Recurring rewards applied monthly on subscription anniversary.

**Q: What if my referral doesn't use my link but mentions my name?**
A: Contact referrals@disclosurely.com with details. We can manually attribute if within 30 days and verifiable.

**Q: Can I refer multiple departments in same organization?**
A: No, only one referral reward per organization (based on primary account). Departments should use same organization account.

**Q: Do rewards expire?**
A: Account credits never expire. Storage and free months expire as noted at redemption.

**Q: Can I transfer credits to another organization?**
A: No, rewards are non-transferable and tied to your account.

**Q: What if my referral cancels?**
A: Recurring rewards stop when they cancel. Initial referral reward is not clawed back unless fraud is detected.
