# Subscription Tiers & Pricing

Choose the right plan for your organization's whistleblowing and compliance program needs.

## Available Plans

### Free Plan

**Perfect for:** Small teams testing the platform

**Features:**
- Up to 10 reports per month
- 1 GB storage
- 3 team members
- Basic analytics
- Email support
- 7-day data retention

**Price:** $0/month

### Starter Plan (tier1)

**Perfect for:** Growing organizations (50-200 employees)

**Features:**
- Up to 50 reports per month
- 10 GB storage
- 10 team members
- Full analytics dashboard
- [Custom branding](/admin/custom-branding)
- [Assignment rules](/features/assignment-rules)
- [SLA management](/features/sla-management)
- Priority email support
- 1-year data retention

**Price:** Contact sales

### Pro Plan (tier2)

**Perfect for:** Enterprise organizations (200+ employees)

**Features:**
- Unlimited reports
- 100 GB storage (expandable)
- Unlimited team members
- Advanced analytics & reporting
- [Custom domains](/admin/custom-domains)
- [AI case analysis](/ai/case-helper)
- [Pattern detection](/ai/pattern-detection)
- [Workflow automation](/features/workflow-engine)
- Dedicated support
- Unlimited data retention
- [GDPR request management](/features/gdpr-requests)
- [Audit trail](/features/audit-trail)
- White-label branding

**Price:** Contact sales
