# Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about Disclosurely answered.

## General

**Q: What is Disclosurely?**
A: Disclosurely is an enterprise whistleblowing and compliance management platform with end-to-end encryption, AI-powered analysis, and comprehensive compliance tools.

**Q: Who can use Disclosurely?**
A: Any organization that needs secure whistleblowing channels - companies, non-profits, government agencies, schools, healthcare providers, and more.

**Q: How much does it cost?**

## Security & Privacy

**Q: How secure is Disclosurely?**
A: Military-grade AES-256 encryption, zero-knowledge architecture, ISO 27001 certified. See [Security](/security/encryption).

**Q: Can Disclosurely see my reports?**
A: No. Zero-knowledge encryption means we cannot access report content. Only authorized users in your organization can decrypt reports.

**Q: Is anonymity really guaranteed?**
A: Yes. Anonymous reports collect no personal information and are encrypted client-side. We cannot identify reporters.

**Q: What if I lose my tracking ID?**
A: Anonymous reports cannot be recovered if tracking ID is lost (by design for security). Confidential reports can be recovered by contacting support.

## Reporting

**Q: How do I submit a report?**
A: Access your organization's secure reporting link and follow the submission process. See [How to Submit](/reporting/how-to-submit).

**Q: Should I report anonymously or confidentially?**
A: Anonymous for maximum privacy. Confidential if you're comfortable sharing identity for easier communication. See [Report Types](/reporting/report-types).

**Q: How long does investigation take?**
A: Typically 2-4 weeks, varies by complexity. Check your tracking ID for updates.

**Q: Will I know the outcome?**
A: Yes, you'll receive status updates and final outcome (within confidentiality limits). Specific disciplinary actions may not be shared.

## For Administrators

**Q: How do I set up my organization?**
A: See [Initial Setup Guide](/admin/initial-setup).

**Q: Can I customize the branding?**
A: Yes, on Pro and Enterprise plans. See [Custom Branding](/admin/custom-branding).

**Q: How do I invite team members?**
A: Navigate to Dashboard > Team > Invite. See [Team Management](/admin/team-management).

**Q: What user roles are available?**
A: Admin, Org Admin, Case Handler, and Reviewer. See [Team Management](/admin/team-management).

## Billing

**Q: Can I cancel anytime?**
A: Yes, cancel anytime. Access continues until end of billing cycle.

**Q: Do you offer refunds?**
A: 14-day money-back guarantee on first Pro subscription.

**Q: Is there a discount for annual billing?**
A: Yes, 20% discount for annual billing.

## Compliance

**Q: Is Disclosurely GDPR compliant?**
A: Yes, fully GDPR compliant with automated data request processing.

**Q: Does it meet EU Whistleblowing Directive requirements?**
A: Yes, designed specifically for EU Directive compliance.

**Q: What audit capabilities are included?**
A: Tamper-evident audit logs with hash chain integrity for all actions.

## Support

**Q: How do I get help?**
A: Email support@disclosurely.com, use in-app chat, or browse this documentation.

**Q: What are your support hours?**
A: Monday-Friday 9am-5pm GMT. Enterprise customers get 24/7 support.

**Q: Do you offer training?**
A: Yes, documentation, video tutorials, and live training for Enterprise customers.
