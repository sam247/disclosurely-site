Subscription & Billing ​

Manage your Disclosurely subscription, billing information, and usage in one place.

Subscription Plans ​

Basic (Free) ​

  • 10 reports per month
  • Core reporting features
  • Basic case management
  • Email notifications
  • 30-day data retention

Pro (£39.99/month) ​

  • Unlimited reports
  • Advanced case management
  • AI-powered analysis
  • Custom branding
  • Custom domains
  • Compliance management
  • GDPR automation
  • Priority support

Enterprise (Custom) ​

  • Everything in Pro
  • Dedicated support
  • Custom integrations
  • SLA guarantees
  • Volume discounts
  • Annual billing
  • Training included

Viewing Your Subscription ​

Navigate to Settings > Billing to view:

  • Current plan
  • Billing cycle
  • Next billing date
  • Usage statistics
  • Invoice history
  • Payment method

Upgrading Your Plan ​

Upgrade to Pro ​

  1. Go to Settings > Billing
  2. Click "Upgrade to Pro"
  3. Enter payment information
  4. Review pricing (£39.99/month)
  5. Click "Confirm Upgrade"

What Happens:

  • Immediate access to Pro features
  • Prorated charge for current month
  • Billing cycle starts from upgrade date

Upgrade to Enterprise ​

  1. Click "Contact Sales" in billing settings
  2. Complete inquiry form
  3. Schedule consultation call
  4. Receive custom quote
  5. Sign agreement
  6. Account upgraded

Downgrading Your Plan ​

To downgrade from Pro to Basic:

  1. Navigate to Settings > Billing
  2. Click "Change Plan"
  3. Select Basic
  4. Confirm downgrade
  5. Takes effect at end of billing cycle

Important:

  • Access to Pro features until cycle ends
  • Custom branding removed
  • Custom domains disabled
  • AI features locked
  • Report history retained

Managing Payment ​

Update Payment Method ​

  1. Go to Settings > Billing
  2. Click "Update Payment Method"
  3. Enter new card details
  4. Click "Save"
  5. New method used for next billing

Payment Methods Accepted ​

  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
  • Apple Pay
  • Google Pay
  • Bank Transfer (Enterprise, annual billing)

Billing & Invoices ​

View Invoices ​

Access all invoices:

  1. Navigate to Settings > Billing > Invoices
  2. View invoice list with:
    • Invoice number
    • Date
    • Amount
    • Status (Paid, Pending, Failed)
  3. Download PDF for accounting

Failed Payments ​

If payment fails:

  1. Email notification sent
  2. Retry attempt after 3 days
  3. Second retry after 7 days
  4. Account suspended if still failing
  5. Update payment method to resolve

Account Suspended:

  • Reporting portal disabled
  • Cannot access dashboard
  • Data preserved
  • Reactivated upon payment

Usage & Limits ​

Current Usage ​

View usage statistics:

Reports:

  • Total reports this month
  • Remaining allowance (Basic plan)
  • Historical usage trends

Team Members:

  • Active team members
  • Available seats
  • Additional seat costs

AI Conversations:

  • AI conversations used (Pro plan: 100/month)
  • Remaining allowance
  • Usage history

Storage:

  • Total storage used
  • File upload volumes
  • Retention periods

Exceeding Limits ​

Basic Plan - Report Limit:

  • 10 reports per month included
  • Upgrade to Pro for unlimited
  • Cannot submit reports once limit reached

Pro Plan - Team Seats:

  • 5 seats included
  • Additional seats: £5/user/month
  • Add/remove seats anytime
  • Prorated billing

Canceling Your Subscription ​

To cancel your Pro subscription:

  1. Navigate to Settings > Billing
  2. Click "Cancel Subscription"
  3. Provide feedback (optional)
  4. Confirm cancellation

What Happens:

  • Access continues until cycle ends
  • No further charges
  • Downgraded to Basic at renewal
  • Data preserved for 90 days

Data After Cancellation ​

90-Day Grace Period:

  • All data accessible (read-only)
  • Can export data
  • Can reactivate subscription
  • No charges during grace period

After 90 Days:

  • Data permanently deleted
  • Cannot be recovered
  • Export data before deletion

Billing FAQ ​

Q: Can I get a refund? A: 14-day money-back guarantee on first Pro subscription. Contact support within 14 days.

Q: Do you offer discounts? A: Annual billing: 20% discount. Non-profits: Contact sales for special pricing.

Q: Can I pause my subscription? A: No, but you can cancel and reactivate anytime within 90 days.

Q: What happens if I don't pay? A: Account suspended after failed retry attempts. Data preserved for 90 days.

Q: Can I pay annually? A: Yes! Annual billing available with 20% discount. Contact sales.
